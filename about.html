Pathology Engagement in Global Health

About Us:

  • Mission: The Global Health Pathology Network facilitates ACVP and its members in providing resources and guidance to enable sustainable, suitable and relevant efforts to global health.

  • Lines of Effort (LOE):
    • Informational and guidance forums
    • Link to find, volunteer, partner and sponsor to/from ACVP
    • Partner with educational programs to support outreach initiatives
  • Endstate: The ACVP GHPN provides the foundation and connections to enable members in pathology activities in support of global health.
